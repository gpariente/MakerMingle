Beach Style Decor

Transform your home with this beach style decor project. It's perfect for bringing a bit of summer into any space!

Materials Needed

  • Sea shells
  • Driftwood
  • String lights

Steps

  1. Arrange sea shells and driftwood on a wooden board.
  2. Wrap the string lights around the arrangement.
  3. Hang the decor piece on a wall to add a beach vibe to your room.
